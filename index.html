About

Shaoyi Wang is a Master of Science in Architecture student studying at the College of Environmental Design, UC Berkeley. Her research interest focuses on innovative structures and computational design, aiming to integrate lightweight structures, parametric design, and digital fabrication into mainstream architectural practices. She is worked as a Graduate Student Instructor (GSI) for a graduate- level course “Introduction to Structures” at UC Berkeley.

Before studying at UC Berkeley, Shaoyi received her Bachelor of Architecture degree, alongside a minor in Psychology, from Tsinghua University, China, in 2023. She won the Integrated Excellence Scholarship of Tsinghua University from 2018 to 2022. Shaoyi's professional experience includes an internship at Song Und Partners Atelier in 2022, where she played a significant role in the facade design of the Kesi Fabric Pavilion at the Dingzhou Garden Expo Park with her parametric design skills. She also worked at China Architecture Design and Research Group as a design intern in 2021.
